About

  • History

    • Japan has a rich history, which started 2600 years ago, with a lot of difrent stages and rulers, also with a lot of culture mixed into it.

  • Culture

    • Japan has, alongside its rich history also a very developed culture dating back to the 8th century that, because of its distance from europe and other great powers, evolved independently.

  • Economy

    • Japan has the 4th largest economy in the world. It consists of primarily computer production and robot parts manifacturing.

  • Passport

    • Japan has a one of the most powerful passports in the world, ranking the 2nd on the global scale. With the Japanesse pasport you can visit 193 countries around the world.
